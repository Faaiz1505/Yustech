FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions

How do I register for JAMB through Yustech?

Simply visit our office or contact us via WhatsApp. We will guide you through the entire JAMB registration process, including profile creation, UTME registration, and result checking. We also assist with change of course/institution when needed.

What types of websites do you develop?

We develop all types of websites including business websites, e-commerce stores, educational portals, blogs, landing pages, and custom web applications. All our websites are responsive, SEO-optimized, and built with modern technologies.

How long are the training programs?

Our training programs range from 2 to 8 weeks depending on the course. HTML/CSS takes 2-3 weeks, JavaScript takes 4 weeks, and Full-Stack Development takes 8 weeks. Each program includes hands-on projects and certification.

Do you offer online training?

Yes! We offer both in-person and online training options. Our online classes are conducted via Zoom with interactive sessions, recorded materials, and one-on-one mentorship support.

What is your pricing for website development?

Our pricing depends on the complexity and features of your website. Basic websites start from affordable rates, while e-commerce and custom applications are priced based on requirements. Contact us for a free quote!

Can you help with admission processing for any university?

Yes, we provide admission processing assistance for all Nigerian universities, polytechnics, and colleges of education. We help with JAMB registration, Post-UTME applications, Direct Entry, and admission follow-up.

How do I start selling on Temu with your help?

Our Temu consultancy service guides you through store setup, product listing optimization, pricing strategy, and sales management. We help you navigate the platform to maximize your earning potential.

Do you offer installment payment for training?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for our training programs. You can pay in installments to make learning more accessible. Contact us to discuss a payment plan that works for you.
